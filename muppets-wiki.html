Some Stuff About Muppets!

Name Description Image
Kermit He is a frog that can sing and dance. Although it is not easy, he is green. His character was created in 1955, and he was originally voiced by Jim Henson.
Gonzo He is a whatever that loves being shot from cannons, falling from heights, and doing other daredevil stuff.
Fozzie He wears a hat because he is a bear, and he loved driving his uncle's old Studebaker.
Miss Piggy This pig is in love with kermit. She also knows karate, and her character was voiced by Frank Oz, who also voiced Yoda.
Lew Zealand He loves throwing fish everywhere, and he always has paper towels when you need them.
